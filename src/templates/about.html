About

Oceantober is a nonprofit dedicated to providing cleaner oceans and building community through beach clean ups and the "Oceantober Challenge" where participants jump in the ocean for all 31 days of October.

In the future we plan on hosting beach cleanups throughout the month of October all along the entire coast of California. We want to spread the joy of Oceantober and encourage people get out in nature as well as support and respect it.
