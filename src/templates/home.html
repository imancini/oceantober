What We Do

Marine Conservation

At each of our daily dips in October, we strive to better the beaches that we visit. This ranges from hosting beach cleanups, to promoting policies and habits that limit waste and pollution reaching the ocean.

Outdoor Education

Each week of Oceantober we hold a community gathering highlighting ocean topics such as marine conservation efforts, the effects of sea level rise, and local community efforts to protect the ocean.

Community Building

Every day of October we invite anyone and everyone to join us in Oceantober to feel the thrill of jumping in that chilly October water. Maybe you will meet a new friend, or learn something new, either way, we are glad to have you join us!
